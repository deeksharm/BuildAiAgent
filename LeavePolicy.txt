ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd.

===========================
LEAVE POLICY
===========================

Casual Leave:
12 days per calendar year.

Sick Leave:
8 days per calendar year.

Earned Leave:
15 days per calendar year.

Maternity Leave:
26 weeks as per company policy.

Paternity Leave:
10 working days.

Marriage Leave:
5 working days.

Bereavement Leave:
3 working days.

Leave Approval:
All leave requests must be approved by the reporting manager.

Half-Day Leave:
Employees may apply for half-day leave.

Leave Encashment:
Unused earned leave may be encashed as per company policy.

Public Holidays:
The company follows the official holiday calendar.

Leave Application:
Employees should apply leave through the HR Portal.

Emergency Leave:
Employees should inform their manager immediately.

Leave Carry Forward:
Maximum 10 earned leaves can be carried forward to the next year.
