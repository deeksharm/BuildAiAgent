ABC BANK
KYC POLICY

Document Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 01 January 2026

Purpose

Know Your Customer (KYC) is mandatory for all banking customers.

Identity Documents

Any one of the following:

• Aadhaar Card
• Passport
• Voter ID
• Driving License

Address Proof

Any one:

• Aadhaar Card
• Utility Bill
• Passport
• Bank Statement

PAN Requirement

PAN Card is mandatory for:

• Opening Savings Account
• Opening Current Account
• Applying for Loans
• Applying for Credit Cards

KYC Verification

Verification methods include:

• Physical Verification
• Video KYC
• Aadhaar e-KYC

KYC Update

Customers must update KYC information every 10 years or whenever personal information changes.

Failure to Update

Failure to complete KYC may result in:

• Temporary account restrictions
• Transaction limitations
• Account freeze as per RBI regulations
