ABC BANK
FRAUD PREVENTION POLICY

Document Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 01 January 2026

Purpose

ABC Bank is committed to preventing financial fraud and protecting customer information.

Common Fraud Types

• Phishing Emails
• Fake Phone Calls
• Fake Loan Offers
• OTP Fraud
• UPI Fraud
• Identity Theft

Security Guidelines

Never share:

• OTP
• ATM PIN
• CVV
• Internet Banking Password
• Mobile Banking Password

Bank employees will never ask for these details.

Customer Responsibilities

Customers should:

• Verify SMS alerts
• Enable transaction notifications
• Report suspicious activities immediately

Fraud Reporting

Emergency Helpline:
1800-999-111

Email:
fraud@abcbank.com

Response Timeline

Fraud complaints are acknowledged within 24 hours.

Investigation usually completes within 7 working days.

Account Blocking

Accounts may be temporarily blocked if suspicious activity is detected.

Security Recommendations

• Change passwords every 90 days.
• Enable Multi-Factor Authentication.
• Never click unknown links.
