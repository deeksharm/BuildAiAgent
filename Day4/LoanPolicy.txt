HOME LOAN POLICY

Document Version: 1.0
Effective Date: 01 January 2026

Overview
ABC Bank provides Home Loans to eligible customers for purchasing residential properties.

Eligibility Criteria

• Applicant must be at least 21 years old.
• Maximum age at loan maturity should not exceed 65 years.
• Applicant must be an Indian citizen.
• Salaried and self-employed applicants are eligible.

Documents Required

• Aadhaar Card
• PAN Card
• Passport-size Photographs
• Last 6 Months Bank Statements
• Last 3 Salary Slips (for salaried employees)
• Form 16 or Income Tax Returns
• Property Documents

Loan Amount

Minimum Loan:
₹5,00,000

Maximum Loan:
₹5,00,00,000

Loan Tenure

Minimum:
5 Years

Maximum:
30 Years

Interest Rate

Starting from 8.40% per annum.
Interest rates are subject to applicant credit score and RBI guidelines.

Loan Processing Time

Average processing time is 7 working days after successful document verification.

Prepayment Rules

• Partial prepayment is allowed.
• No foreclosure charges for floating rate home loans.
• Fixed-rate loans may attract foreclosure charges as per RBI norms.

Reasons for Loan Rejection

• Low Credit Score
• Incomplete Documentation
• Negative Banking History
• Existing High Loan Obligations
• Property Legal Issues
