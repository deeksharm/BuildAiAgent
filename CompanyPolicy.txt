EMPLOYEE POLICY

Office Timing:
Employees are expected to work from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM from Monday to Friday.

Working Days:
Monday to Friday

Weekly Off:
Saturday and Sunday

Casual Leave:
Employees are entitled to 12 days of Casual Leave every calendar year.

Sick Leave:
Employees are entitled to 8 days of Sick Leave every calendar year.

Earned Leave:
Employees receive 15 days of Earned Leave every year.

Work From Home:
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Notice Period:
The notice period for permanent employees is 30 days.

Laptop Policy:
Every employee will receive a company laptop during onboarding.

Dress Code:
Business Casual from Monday to Thursday.
Friday is Smart Casual.

Medical Insurance:
Health insurance is provided to all permanent employees.

Travel Policy:
Employees traveling for business will be reimbursed for approved expenses.

Employee ID Card:
Employees must wear their ID card inside the office premises.
