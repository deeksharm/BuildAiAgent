ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd.

===========================
EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
===========================

Welcome to ABC Technologies.

Employees are expected to maintain professionalism and integrity.

Code of Conduct:
1. Respect colleagues.
2. Maintain confidentiality.
3. Follow company policies.
4. Avoid harassment or discrimination.

Attendance Policy:
Employees should report to work on time.
Late arrivals must be informed to the reporting manager.

Performance Review:
Performance reviews are conducted every six months.

Training:
Employees are encouraged to attend technical and soft skill training sessions.

Probation:
New employees will undergo a probation period of six months.

Resignation:
Employees must submit a formal resignation email to HR.

Assets:
All company assets must be returned before the last working day.

Internet Usage:
Office internet should be used only for official work.

Meeting Etiquette:
Join meetings on time.
Keep microphones muted when not speaking.

Office Facilities:
Cafeteria timings:
8:00 AM to 7:00 PM

Parking:
Parking is available for employees free of cost.

Emergency Contact:
security@abctech.com
